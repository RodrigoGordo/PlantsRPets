About Us

What is Plants R’ Pets?

Plants R’ Pets is a gamified crop management app designed to help users create sustainable habits while growing and caring for their plants. By registering plantations, tracking their progress, and engaging in sustainability challenges, users unlock unique pet companions inspired by the plants they cultivate.

Our Mission

We believe in making sustainability fun, engaging, and rewarding. Our goal is to encourage people to grow their own plants, reduce their environmental footprint, and learn about eco-friendly practices in an interactive way.
