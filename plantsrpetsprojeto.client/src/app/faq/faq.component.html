Frequently Asked Questions

How do I manage my plants?

Simply add your plants to your plantation list, and we'll send you reminders when it's time to water and take care of them.

How can I add pets to my collection?

You can add pets by navigating to the Pet Collections section and filling out a simple form for each pet.

Do I need an internet connection to use the app?

Yes, an internet connection is required to sync your data and receive notifications.

Is the app free?

The app is free to use with basic features. Premium features are available via a subscription.
