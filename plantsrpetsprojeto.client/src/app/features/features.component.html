Features

Grow and track your plants

  • Register your plantations and receive personalized care reminders.
  • Track watering, fertilization, and harvest progress.
  • View seasonal planting recommendations.

Collect unique pets

  • Unlock adorable pets based on the plants you cultivate.
  • Showcase your favorite pets on your profile.

Sustainability insights and tips

  • Get personalized sustainability tips to improve your eco-friendly habits.
  • Access tutorials on composting, sustainable farming and many more!
  • View metrics on water saved and carbon footprint reduction.

Join communities and connect

  • Create or join communities of like-minded plant lovers.
  • Share your plantations and pets in community chats.
  • Challenge friends to friendly pet battles.

Customize Your Profile

  • Add a profile picture, description, and showcase your best plants and pets.
  • Select up to 5 favorite pets and plantations to display on your profile.
